Here Are the Best "It Girl" Brands Every Girl Should Know About

New brands and clothing drops are introduced to the fashion industry on the regular. Adding to the mix of social media’s influence on product recommendations, it can become overwhelming when deciding where to shop. After purchasing from hundreds of brands, I have compiled a recommendations list for some of the latest and trendiest clothing brands for every occasion.

WHITE FOX BOUTIQUE

Shop White Fox Boutque Here

White Fox Boutique is a popular Australian clothing brand, selling clothes, shoes, and accessories. With WFB weekly drops, you can shop the latest trends and styles as seen on influencers and celebrities. Popularized for their loungewear sets, they also offer a wide range of clothing styles, from athleisure wear to going-out outfits. While physical store locations are not yet available in the U.S., you can shop with them at their official website and app where they offer international shipping. Price point-wise, they are quite affordable, with an average price range of $40-$100.

TIGER MIST

Shop Tiger Mist Here

Tiger Mist is a well-known Australian fashion brand. An online destination for the latest fashion trends, they offer a wide range of trendy styles for every occasion. Whether you need an outfit for school, vacation, or going out, Tiger Mist has it. Unlike many other fashion brands, they specialize in creating unique yet trendy pieces, patterns, and colorways that make them stand out in an oversaturated market. While physical store locations are not yet available in the U.S., you can shop with them at their official website where they offer international shipping. Price point-wise, average price ranges from $50-$125, but they incorporate great promotions and sales.

I.AM.GIA

Shop I.AM.GIA Here

I.am.gia is an iconic Australian fashion brand. They specialize in creating statement wardrobe essentials and bold collections of edgy pieces for every season and occasion. With I.am.gia’s rise in popularity over the years, the brand is often worn by celebrities and featured in music videos and tv shows like Euphoria. While physical store locations are not yet available in the U.S., you can shop with them at their official website where they offer fast and easy international shipping. In addition, they have pop-up shops in major cities, like LA. Despite their relatively high price point, the quality, design, and unique pieces are well worth the cost.

SKIMS

Shop SKIMS Here

Skims is Kim Kardashians fashion line. Founded as a solutions-oriented brand, they sell high-quality loungewear, shapewear, and undergarments. Creating staple wardrobe pieces that are trendy yet comfortable, it has created a large fan base, especially on social media. You can shop with them at their official website where they offer international shipping. Skims can also be found in stores and online at Nordstrom, Saks Fifth Avenue, and Harrods. While the price point is relatively expensive, the quality, material, and performance of Skims pieces are well worth the cost.

OH POLLY

Shop Oh Polly Here

Oh Polly is a popular Australian fashion brand. Well-known and loved for their beautiful dress designs, they also offer a wide range of clothing, like everyday basics, festival fits, date night attire, and formal wear. While physical store locations are not yet available in the U.S., you can shop with them at their official website and app where they offer international shipping. Price point-wise, they are relatively expensive, with an average price range of $70-$200.
